Explore Elevate PRO Features

AI-Powered Coaching

  • Custom Workout Routines: Instantly generate workouts based on your goals, time availability, equipment access, and personal preferences.
  • Personalized Recipes: Create meals tailored to your taste, fitness goals, calorie and macro targets, dietary needs, and favorite cuisines.
  • AI-Curated Plans: From dynamic workouts to personalized meal recipes, our AI builds routines and nutrition plans that adapt to your lifestyle, goals, and progress — all in one place.

Advanced Analytics

  • Muscle-Specific Tracking: Track sets per muscle group with customizable time ranges and intervals, visualized through intuitive graphs.
  • Exercise Stats Tracking: Log and review detailed exercise data—weight, reps, predicted one-rep max, set volume, and duration—across any custom time range.
  • Nutrition & Hydration Tracking: Log and analyze calories, macronutrients, micronutrients, and water intake to stay aligned with your goals.

Monthly and Yearly Reports

  • Workout Performance Stats: Analyze total sets, duration, volume, and number of workouts, sets per muscle groups and distributions, along with how they compare to previous periods.
  • Exercise Progress Highlights: See personal records, top-performing exercises, and which movements have improved the most over time as well as your most trained exercises and muscles.
  • Nutrition Breakdown: View insights into calorie, macro and micronutrient intake, and the days you were the closest to your targets.
